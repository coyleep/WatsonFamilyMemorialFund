About Bill Watson

My current wrestling career has been wrestling at the JMU club wrestling team. Its my first year here and I am planning on wrestling between 135-141. While I have no idea how the competition is, I enjoy practice heavily and love learning new information. I am exicted for our first tournament in two week and can't wait to get onto the mat.

The team right now consists of 1 past national finalist and two all american coaches. This means the knowledge I can obtain here could help me become a future all american (hopfully). I am just excited to be part of the team and I am ready for the next step in my wrestling career to start.
